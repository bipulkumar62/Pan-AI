Pan AI is an AI-powered food scanning and nutrition assistant mobile app that helps users understand food instantly. By using the phone camera, users can scan meals, snacks, ingredients, or packaged products and receive smart insights about health value, nutrition quality, and better eating choices.

The goal of Pan AI is to make food knowledge simple, fast, and useful for everyday users.

Main Features
1. AI Food Scanner
Scan food using the mobile camera
Detect meals, fruits, snacks, ingredients, and packaged foods
Instant AI-based analysis
2. Good & Bad Food Analysis

Pan AI explains:

Healthy benefits of the food
Possible unhealthy factors
Sugar levels
Protein value
Fat quality
Fiber content
Processed food warnings
3. Smart Nutrition Insights

Instead of confusing data, the app gives simple explanations such as:

Good for muscle gain
High sugar, eat in moderation
Low protein option
Better for energy than recovery
4. Leftover Food Recipe Generator

Users can scan available ingredients or leftover food and get:

Recipe suggestions
Meal combinations
Snack ideas
Ways to reduce food waste
